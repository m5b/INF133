Ubiquitous Computing

  • Weiser speculated people would interact with three types of computers:
    1. Tabs: inch-scale devices, like post-its
    2. Pads: foot-scale devices, like paper
    3. Boards: yard-scale devices, like whiteboards
  • Also speculated devices would have shared ownership
How does Weiser's speculation hold up today?
3

Ubiquitous Computing

  • Mostly lines up with what we use today:
    1. Tabs: phones and watches
    2. Pads: tablets and laptops
    3. Boards: projectors? smart TV's?, AR/VR?
  • Devices still have strong sense of ownership, not speculated shared ownership
